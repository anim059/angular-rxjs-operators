What is the Tap Operator?

The Tap operator in RxJS is a utility operator that allows you to perform side effects (such as logging, debugging, or modifying external state) without altering the values emitted by the source observable. It is often used for debugging, logging, or triggering actions based on observable emissions. Used for performing side effects.

Common Use Cases:

  • Logging and Debugging: Log values at different stages of an observable chain.
  • Triggering Side Effects: Perform actions like updating the UI or external state.
  • Inspecting Values: Inspect values without altering the observable chain.
